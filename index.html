ABOUT

Resolute is an evening event where designers, developers, motion graphic & film students showcase their work.

Humber resolute is a showcase for the multimedia design and development 2018 graduating class of Humber college. Up and coming designers, developers and multimedia artists coming together to showcase the final drafts of their portfolios that include works that range from web design, mobile interface design, experience design, motion graphics, web coding, user testing, video and sound editing, user interface prototyping and interactive design. The Humber resolute portfolio is where you’ll find the best of the best in emerging design, development and multimedia professionals.
